### INVEST Principles Validation

All user stories have been validated against INVEST principles:

- ✅ **Independent**: Stories can be developed and delivered independently, dependencies are explicitly noted
- ✅ **Negotiable**: Stories describe intent rather than technical implementation, leaving design space for development team
- ✅ **Valuable**: Each story has a clear "so that" value proposition, directly addressing pain points from user journeys
- ✅ **Estimable**: Story scope is clear with assigned story points using Fibonacci sequence
- ✅ **Small**: Maximum story points is [XX], deliverable within 1-2 sprints
- ✅ **Testable**: Each story has clear acceptance criteria to verify completion
